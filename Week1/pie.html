Straight-Up Rhubarb Pie


Cooking Time 1 hour 15 minutes

This rhubarb pie contains no distractions, like strawberries. The crust is made with shortening. (Butter is fine if you want a French tart, but it's not American pie unless it's made with shortening, the author Anne Dimock said.) The top is marked with 8 razor-thin vents. --Amanda Hesser
